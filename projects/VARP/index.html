Analysis Template

Describe the two images given to you, which includes final frames with sketch of trajectory for the goal [task description] from the MetaWorld simulation environment.
The sketch image on last frame features a simple, 3D-rendered scene. Notably, a trajectory is sketched represented by a thin, yellow color.
The trajectory's color uses yellow to indicate temporal progress, with brighter yellow signifies the beginning of the trajectory and darker brown color signifies the end, together representing a temporal progression from yellow to dark color.

1. How is the sketch shown in Image 1?
2. How is the sketch shown in Image 2?
3. Is there any difference between Image 1 and Image 2 in terms of achieving the goal?
